How I Started My Web Development Journey

Written by Shiv Kumar

Monday · Jan 22, 2026

Web development is not just about writing code, it is about solving problems and creating experiences. When I started, everything felt confusing, but consistency helped me move forward.

Learning HTML and CSS gave me confidence. Slowly, JavaScript opened the doors to logic, interactivity and real-world applications.

Start With Basics

Mastering the basics makes advanced topics easier. Focus on concepts, not just syntax.

Consistency is Key

Even 30 minutes of daily practice can bring massive improvement over time. Growth is slow but real.
